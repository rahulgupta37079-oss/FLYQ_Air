## 🎬 **Animations & Effects**

### **CSS Animations (No JavaScript!):**

1. **@keyframes animate-float** (6s duration)
   - Drone floats up/down with slight rotation
   - Smooth ease-in-out
   - Infinite loop

2. **@keyframes animate-float-slow** (8s duration)
   - Slower floating for feature drone
   - Subtle movement
   - Adds depth

3. **@keyframes ping-slow** (3s duration)
   - Ring expands and fades
   - Creates radar effect
   - Green colored ring

4. **@keyframes scroll-indicator** (1.5s)
   - Scroll arrow bounces
   - Fades out at bottom
   - Invites user interaction

5. **@keyframes fade-in** (1s)
   - Elements fade in on load
   - Slides up 20px
   - Professional entrance

### **Hover Interactions:**

## 🎨 **Design Philosophy**

### **Why GREEN works better:**
1. **Fresh & Innovative** 🌱
   - Green represents growth and new technology
   - Feel of "new" and "cutting-edge"

2. **Action & Energy** ⚡
   - Green = "go", "ready", "active status"
   - Creates sense of urgency and excitement

3. **Tech & Gaming** 🎮
   - Popular in tech products and gaming
   - Appeals to target audience (makers, developers)

4. **Brand Differentiation** 🚁
   - Most drone companies use blue
   - Green makes FLYQ stand out

5. **Nature & Flight** 🌿
   - Connects to outdoor/nature flying
   - Organic, natural feel

## 📱 **Responsive Design**

### **Desktop (1024px+):**
- Split layout (50/50)
- Large floating drone image
- 4-column feature grid
- Full animations enabled

### **Tablet (768px - 1023px):**
- Stacked layout (text top, image bottom)
- Smaller drone image
- 2-column feature grid
- Reduced animation intensity

### **Mobile (< 768px):**
- Single column layout
- No floating drone (hidden)
- 1-column feature grid
- Essential animations only
