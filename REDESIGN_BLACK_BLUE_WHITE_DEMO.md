## 🎨 **Design Philosophy**

### **Cohesive Theme:**
Every section now follows the **Black, Blue, White** color scheme:
- **Black** = Foundation, elegance, premium feel
- **Blue** = Energy, technology, trust
- **White** = Clarity, contrast, readability

### **Visual Hierarchy:**
1. **Hero** → Dramatic video with blue accents
2. **Products** → Bold cards with blue highlights
3. **Features** → (Not changed yet - can be updated if approved)

### **Brand Identity:**
- **Tech-forward**: Blue represents technology and innovation
- **Premium**: Black creates luxury and sophistication
- **Clean**: White ensures readability and elegance
