### Regular Maintenance

- Check battery health regularly
- Calibrate compass before flights

- Store in cool, dry place

- **Won't take off:** Check battery, calibrate

- **Video lag:** Reduce distance, check WiFi
- **Short flight time:** Replace aging batteries
- **Weak signal:** Extend antennas fully

**Q: Is this drone suitable for beginners?**  
A: Yes! It features one-key takeoff/landing, altitude hold, and headless mode, making it perfect for beginners.

**Q: How long does the battery last?**  
A: Each battery provides 20-25 minutes of flight time. With 2 batteries included, you get 40-50 minutes total.

**Q: Can I use this drone indoors?**  
A: Yes, but we recommend outdoor use for best experience. Use propeller guards for indoor flying.

**Q: What's the maximum flying distance?**  
A: Control range is 80-100 meters. WiFi FPV range is 50-80 meters.

**Q: Do I need a license to fly this drone in India?**  
A: For recreational use under 250g, no license is required. Check local regulations for specific areas.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What's the difference between FLYQ Consumer and FLYQ Vision?**  
A: FLYQ Vision is programmable with ESP32-S3 for developers. Consumer is ready-to-fly for everyone.

**Q: Can I program this drone?**  
A: No, this is a consumer-ready drone. For programmable drones, check FLYQ Air or FLYQ Vision.

**Q: Does it have GPS?**  
A: This model uses altitude hold and visual positioning. GPS models coming soon.

**Q: Can I replace parts?**  
A: Yes! Spare propellers are included, and other parts are available for purchase.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**  
A: Yes, available for both iOS and Android with FPV, controls, and settings.
