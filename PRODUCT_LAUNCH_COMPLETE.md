**Q: Can I fly it indoors?**  
A: Yes, but outdoor use is recommended. Consider propeller guards for indoor flying.

**Q: How long does the battery last?**  
A: Each battery: 20-25 minutes. Total with 2 batteries: 40-50 minutes.

A: Control range: 80-100 meters. WiFi FPV range: 50-80 meters.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**  
A: Yes! Available for both iOS and Android with FPV streaming, controls, and settings.

**Q: Can I replace damaged parts?**  
A: Yes! Spare propellers are included. Other parts available for purchase.
